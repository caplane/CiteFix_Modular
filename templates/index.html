About CiteFix Pro

CiteFix Pro is a deterministic document remediation engine designed for historians, legal scholars, and academics.

The Router

Detects mixed content (URLs, Books, Legal Cases) within a single footnote and splits them for parallel processing.

Extraction Engines

Uses deterministic mapping (for Government/Legal) and API queries (for Books) to retrieve authoritative metadata.

Unlike generative AI, CiteFix uses strict heuristic rules to identify sources and reformat them into Chicago Manual of Style (17th Ed.), preserving your document's structure and active hyperlinks.
